Mwapokeleleka mu Wikipedia, nkhokwe ya vinjelu yaulele ya m'chiTumbuka. Pano tina nkhani zakukwana 1,225 articles. 'Pano, waliyose wangasazgapo ivyo wakumanya vyakuti tose tingasambilako kuti chiyowoyelo chithu chilute m'nthazi. Nkhokwe ya Wikipedia ya m'chiNgelezi, yikayambika mu chaka cha 2001 ndipo yiri na vyakulemba vyakujumpha million - banyithu bakulemba-tu, ise kee?' Imwe bayowoyi ba chiTumbuka na viyowoyero vinyakhe vya ku Mala?i, kumafumilo ya zuba ya Zambia na ku mwela kwa Tanzania imwe namwe mungalembapo! Yambani sono kulutizga m'nthazi kumanya vinthu! ABRAHAM LINCOLN: Abraham Lincoln Abraham Lincoln (Febuluwale 12, 1809 ? Epulelo 15, 1865) wakawa prezidenti wa nambala 16,wa chalo cha United States of America kufuma mu cha chaka cha1861 mpaka mu chaka icho wakakomekela mu chaka cha 1865. Pama prezidenti ghose gha chalo cha United States,uyo yekha ndiye walikupokela ntchindi za kuluska chifukwa ndiye wakamazga ukapolo( slavery),kuyambiska umoza wa vyalo vakupambana (union) kweneso wakalutiska chuma cha chalo chake panthazi. MBIRI YAKE MWAKUDUMULA Lincoln wakababika mu mwezi wa Febuluwale ,pa 12, mu chaka cha 1809,mwana wachiwili wa Thomas Lincoln na Nancy Hanks Lincoln, mu nyumba ya chidimbo pa munda wa Sinking Spring pafupi na Hodgenville, Kentucky.
