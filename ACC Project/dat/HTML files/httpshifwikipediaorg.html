Aaj ke featured article

Sashi Mahendra Singh (1920 - 1990), Fiji ke Ba district me ek businessman aur Ba town ke mayor rahaa lekin Fiji bhar ke log uske Ba Soccer Team aur Fiji National Team ke coach se jaane hai. Uu Ba aur Fiji team ke khilaarri aur ek referee bhi rahaa. Foootball ke alawa uu Hockey, Cricket, Tennis, Athletics, Boxing aur Rugby me bhi aapan yogdaan diis rahaa. Aapan politician, pitaji, Chattur Singh, ke sujhao pe Fiji ke kuchh Hindustani me se ek rahaa jon ki Second World War me army me bharti hoe gais rahaa. Iske baad uu aapan business suruu karis jisme auctioning aur motar ke spare parts ke business rahaa. Uu Ba Township Board ke Chairman bhi rahaa. (aur...)


Aap janta hai...
Ratu Cakobau
  • 1875 ke measles epidemic, jisme 20% Kaiviti logan mar gae rahin, ke Fiji me failae waala, Ratu Cakobau aur uske dui larrka logan rahin jon ki March 1875 me Sydney se ghuum ke lauta rahin.
  • 1879 se lae ke 1916 tak, 42 jahaaj, 87 trip me 60,553 girmitya logan ke lae ke Fiji se aais rahaa. Isme se 45,439 log Calcutta se aur 15,114 log Madras se jahaaj me charrhin rahaa.
  • Fiji ke Legislative Council ke pahila Hindustani member Badri Maharaj rahaa, jiske Governor nominate karis rahaa aur jon 1917 - 1923 aur 1926 - 1929 tak Council ke member rahaa.
  • Fiji me pahila football competition Nasau Park, Levuka me 1924 me, Cession ke golden jublee celebrate kare ke time, chhe Kaiviti team ke biich me Ricarnie Cup khatir, khela gais rahaa.
  • Fiji ke Hindustani ke army me join kare ke khatir, 1934 me Indian Platoon banawa gais rahaa lekin iske 1940 me disband kar dewa gais rahaa.
  • Royal Air Force ke khatir fighter plane kharide ke khatir, 1943 me ek Fighter Fund suruu karaa gais rahaa, aur ii paisa se nau fighter aur paanch bomber kharida gais rahaa. Ek fighter ke naam Fiji Indian Fighter rahaa.
  • Fiji Trade Union Congress se pahile Ami Chandra, 1950s me Fiji Industrial Workers Congress suruu karis rahaa.
  • Fiji ke 1997 ke constitution me English aur Kaiviti ke saathe Hindustani ke Fiji ke official language banawa gais rahaa.

Taaja khabar
  • 12 November - Fiji ke Solicitor-General, Sharvada Sharma, ke kaam se nikaal ke uske jagah Preetika Prasad ke rakkhaa gais hae.
  • 6 August - High Court ke Judge, Kamal Kumar ke Fiji ke Chief Justice banaawa gais.
  • 13 July - Justin Narayan, jiske mai-baap Fiji me paida bhais rahin, Australian Master Chef Competition ke jiitis
  • 21 February - Suva ke ANZ Stadium me 29th Champion versus Champion (CVC) series Suva aur Labasa ke biich me khela gais. Pahila game 1-1 draw rahaa aur duusra game me Labasa 2-1 se jiit ke CVC supercup ke paanchwa dafe jiitis.
  • 21 January - Kamlesh Prakash ke Fiji khatir India ke nawaa High Commissioner bnaawa gais hai. Prakash pahile UAE ke Ambassadar rahaa aur Delhi me Yogesh Punja ke jagha liis hai.
  • 16 January - United Nations me Fiji ke permanent representative, Nazhat Shameem Khan ke 2021 ke khatir UN Human Rights Council ke president chuna gais.
  • 6 December - Labasa ke Subrail Park me Suva, Nadi ke 1 - 0 se harae ke 2020 ke Football Association Cup Tournament ke jiit liis.
2017 ke Archive

Aaj se sau saal pahile, 1921 me...
  • 1 January - Vashist Muni, Wailialai, Ba me ek meeting karis jisme 3,000 Hindustani logan rahin. Sadhu ji Hindustani log ke batais ke uu logan India laute ke khatir aapan ghar aur jamiin nai beche.
  • 11 February - Ba ke Hindustani majuur apne se harrtaal kar din. Harrtaal puura Weatern Division me fail gais. CSR harrtaal kare waala majuur se milis aur CSR ke 16 demand dewa gais.
  • 23 March - Vashist Muni ke arrest kar dewa gais jab uu Rewa jaawat rahaa, kaaheki sarkar sochat rahaa ki uu harrtaal ke Rewa me barrhae maangat rahaa. Uske Fiji se deport kar dewa gais rahaa. Uske arrest ke dui din baad Suva ke Government House pe bijli giraa aur jar gais.
  • April - Indian Franchise Commission ii sifaris karis ki Hindustani logan dui jan ke Legislative Council ke khatir chune ke mauka mile ke chaahi.
  • 2 April - 245 Girmitya logan jon ki India laut gain rahaa, aur jon ki chaahe Fiji me paida bhain rahaa, nai to, jiske palwaar nai to property Fiji me rahaa, ke sarkar Fiji lautar liis.
  • 24 April - Fiji me census lewa gais aur Fiji ke 157,266 ke abaadi me se 60,634 (36.8%) Hindustani rahin.
  • 31 December - 1921 me 3,795 girmitya logan India laut gain, jis me se 3,183 Calcutta gain aur 612 Madras gain.
