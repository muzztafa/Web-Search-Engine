Muhammad da Hausa Muhammadu, (larabci ?????????) furucci [mu?ammad]; An haife shi c. 570 CE – 8 Juni 632 CE) ya kasance annabi kuma Manzo ne, wato ma'aikin Allah ma?aukakin Sarki, wanda aka aiko domin ya tabbatar da addinin da annabawan da suka gabace shi suka koyar, kamar Annabi Ibrahim da Annabi Musa da Annabi Isah da dukkannin sauran annabawa, (tsira da amincin Allah su kara tabbata a gare su).
