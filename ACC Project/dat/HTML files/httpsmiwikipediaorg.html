Ko Matariki, te whet? o te tau

He k?hui whet? e whitu a Matariki, e m?hiotia nei ko Pleiades. I ia h?toke, ko te putanga mai o Matariki hei whakaatu mai kua pau te tau tawhito, kua t?mata he tau hou. Mai r? an?, ko te putanga mai o Matariki he w? whakanui, he w? whakatikatika hoki m? te tau hou.

He w? whakareri a Matariki i te whenua. I waenganui o te h?toke i te w? o Matariki, e ?hua moe k? ana te whenua. Ka whakat?ngia he hua whenua hei koha ki ng? atua o te whenua. Ka tata te k?anga te tae mai, k?hore atu he w? i te w? o Matariki hei whakatakoto kaupapa m? te whenua, m? ng? mahi whenua hoki o te k?anga ?ianei. He w? pai hoki hei ako i ng? ?huatanga o te whenua me te ngahere.

Kua m?hio r?nei koe...

Ko Meri Te Tai Mangak?hia te wahine tuatahi i t? ki te k?rero ki te P?remata M?ori o Te Kotahitanga? I wh?nau mai ia i te 22 o Mei 1868 i t?tahi w?hi e tata ana ki Panguru i te moana o Te Hokianga. Ko tana t?ne ko H?miora Mangak?hia, he rangatira o Hauraki i tohungia i 1892 hei Pirimia m? te P?remata M?ori, ar? m? te Kotahitanga.

N? te 18 o Mei i te tau 1893 i t? te hui a te P?remata o te Kotahitanga i Heretaunga; ka puta te motini a Meri Te Tai Mangak?hia kia whai w?hi ng? w?hine ki te p?titanga. Ka t? a Meri ki te tautoko ki tana m?tini i roto i te P?remata – ko ia tonu te wahine tuatahi i k?rero ki taua huinga.
