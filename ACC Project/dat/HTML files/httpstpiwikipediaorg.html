King blong Tonga i dai pinis

Praim Minista blong Tonga, itokaut pinis olsem, King blong Tonga na man husat ibin bringim Demokrasi igo long kantri, King George Tupou the fifth,i dai pinis. King Tupou the fifth krismas blong en 63 ibin dai aste long Hong Kong.Yangpla brata blong en ibin stap wantem em taem emi bin dai. King Tupou ino bin marit na brata blong en, Crown Prince, bai kisim ples blong en na kamap olsem nupla King. Niusmeri long Nukua'lofa, Monalisa Palu, ibin tokim Redio Australia, olsem King Tupou ibin wanpla man husat ibin kamapim ol gutpla senis long kantri.
